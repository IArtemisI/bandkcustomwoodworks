Quick Care Basics

These 5 habits keep hardwood looking gorgeous for years.

  • Wash by hand only: warm water + mild soap.
  • No soaking: water is wood’s arch-nemesis.
  • Dry immediately: towel dry, then air dry standing on edge.
  • Avoid extreme heat: no dishwasher, no microwave, no “sunbathing” in the car.
  • Oil/wax regularly: more often in dry seasons or heavy use.
