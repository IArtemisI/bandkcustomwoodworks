Welcome to B&K Custom Woodworks

We are a family-owned shop based in the small town of Wilson, Oklahoma. What started as a simple hobby quickly turned into a passion for keeping traditional woodworking alive. We specialize in handcrafted butcher blocks, but have extensive experience in crafting high end furniture as well. Our products are made with high quality exotic hardwoods that will stand the test of time ensuring they can be shared with future generations.

Available Woods

Purple Heart

Purple heart is a beautiful tropical hardwood that is native to Central and South America. It has a medium texture with purple and red undertones.

Bodark

Bodark is a straight grain hardwood native in the south-central region of the United States. It has a fine to medium texture with a bright yellow to orange hue.

Blood Wood

Blood wood is a striking hardwood that is native to South America. It has a medium to coarse texture with a deep red color that resembles dried blood.

African Padauk

Padauk is a tropical hardwood native to Africa and Southeast Asia. It has a medium to coarse texture with a vibrant reddish-orange color that tends to darken over time.

Zebra Wood

Zebra wood is an exotic hardwood native to West Africa. It has a medium to coarse texture with a distinctive striped pattern resembling that of a zebra.

Black Walnut

Black walnut is a hardwood native to North America. It has a fine to medium texture with a rich dark brown color and beautiful grain patterns.
