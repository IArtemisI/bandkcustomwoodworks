Welcome to B&K Custom Woodworks

We are a family-owned shop based in the small town of Wilson, Oklahoma. What started as a simple hobby quickly turned into a passion for keeping traditional woodworking alive. We specialize in handcrafted butcher blocks, but have extensive experience in crafting high end furniture as well. Our products are made with high quality exotic hardwoods that will stand the test of time ensuring they can be shared with future generations.
