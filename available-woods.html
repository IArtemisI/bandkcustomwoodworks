Available Woods

Purple Heart

Also Known as: Peltogyne, Violet Wood, and Amaranth

Purple heart is a beautiful tropical hardwood that is native to Central and South America. It has a medium texture with purple and red undertones. It is well known for its strength and durability making it a wonderful choice for precious creations that will last a lifetime.

Bodark

Also Known as: Bois d'Arc, Osage Orange, and Horse Apple

Bodark is a straight grain hardwood native in the south-central region of the United States. It has a fine to medium texture with a bright yellow to orange hue. Bodark is extremely dense and durable, making it an excellent choice for items that will see heavy use over time.

Blood Wood

Also Known as: Brazilian Bloodwood

Blood wood is a striking exotic hardwood that is native to South America. It has a medium to coarse texture with a deep red color that resembles dried blood. Blood wood is known for its hardness and durability, making it a popular choice for high-end woodworking projects.
