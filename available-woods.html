Available Woods

Purple Heart

Purple heart is a beautiful tropical hardwood that is native to Central and South America. It has a medium texture with purple and red undertones.

Bodark

Bodark is a straight grain hardwood native in the south-central region of the United States. It has a fine to medium texture with a bright yellow to orange hue.

Blood Wood

Blood wood is a striking exotic hardwood that is native to South America. It has a medium to coarse texture with a deep red color that resembles dried blood.

African Padauk

Padauk is a tropical hardwood native to Africa and Southeast Asia. It has a medium to coarse texture with a vibrant reddish-orange color that tends to darken over time.

Zebra Wood

Zebra wood is a striking exotic hardwood native to West Africa. It has a medium to coarse texture with a distinctive striped pattern resembling that of a zebra.

Black Walnut

Black walnut is a highly sought-after hardwood native to North America. It has a fine to medium texture with a rich dark brown color and beautiful grain patterns.
