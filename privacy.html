Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

B&K Custom Woodworks values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected when you visit our website or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and message details when you contact us or request a custom order.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Communicate about orders or custom projects
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. This information is anonymous and helps us improve performance and usability.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (such as hosting providers) to operate our website. These services are only given access to information necessary to perform their functions.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may request to review, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out through our contact form.
